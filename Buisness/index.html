Order of Analysis

  1. Review year's taxt return [amount owed and witholding]
  2. Go through income return instructions for IT-201 & 1040
  3. Allow some time to pass to collect all money info from job W-2 & money account 1099
  4. Allow a couple of weeks before due date [usually 4/15] to fill out forms
  5. Send money if State or Federal demand is over paid on W-2 w/ additions/subtractions
  6. Modify witholding from job if needed [As appears on W-2]

Federal return gets completed first because many State forms use information from the finalized Federal return.

Federal Taxes

Federal resources: IRS: help

Forms for Individual tax return: Forms Form 1040 is the main form used for individual tax return. Complete this form following instructions. Fill out any additional forms as directed from 1040 instructions

Form Purpose Priority Action Link
1040 Annual income tax return Primary Follow the schedules and instructions for form. Additional forms will be used with this form including copyB of the W-2 About Form 1040
W-2 Report of wages and taxes withheld Primary Use copy B of W-2 to fill out 1040 form. This form is used to report how much money you made and how much tax was withheld from your paycheck. About Form W-2
1099-INT & 1099-DIV Report of income from sources other than wages Important Secondary Use 1099 form to report any additional income you received that is not from a job. This could include interest[1099-INT] or dividends[1099-DIV] from money accounts or freelance work. About Form 1099
Note: The 1040 form is the main form for filing your federal taxes, and the W-2 and 1099 forms are used to report your income. It is important to fill out the 1040 form accurately and completely, using the information from your W-2 and 1099 forms to ensure that you are reporting all of your income correctly.

After Federal return is completed, use information from it to complete State return.

State Taxes

State resources: Department of Tax and Finance: help

Forms for income tax: Forms IT-201 is the resident income tax return. Complete this form following instructions. Fill out any additional forms as directed from IT-201 instructions
The bottom of the page shows Income tax tables used to determine if you overpaid or are short in money paid to state.


Form Purpose Priority Action Link
IT-201 Resident income tax return Primary Follow the schedules and instructions for form. Additional forms will be used with this form including information from 1040 form. About Form IT-201
W-2 Report of wages and witholding tax Primary Use copy 2 of W-2 to fill out IT-201 form. This form is used to report how much money you made and how much tax was withheld from your paycheck. About Form W-2
Note: The IT-201 form is the main form for filing your state taxes, and the W-2 form is used to report your income. It is important to fill out the IT-201 form accurately and completely, using the information from your W-2 form to ensure that you are reporting all of your income correctly to the state.
